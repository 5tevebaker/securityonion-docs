Security Onion Console
======================

In this section, we'll look at different analyst tools that can be used for slicing and dicing data coming from your network and endpoints.

Most of our analyst tools are browser-based. We recommend chromium or chromium-based browsers such as Google Chrome. Other browsers may work, but we recommend chromium-based browsers for best compatibility.
